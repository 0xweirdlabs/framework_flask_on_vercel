Frequently Asked Questions

How does an air fryer work?

Air fryers use rapid air circulation technology to cook food, creating a crispy exterior while keeping the inside moist.

Is air fried food healthy?

Yes, air frying requires significantly less oil than traditional frying methods, resulting in lower fat and calorie content.

What can I cook in an air fryer?

Air fryers are versatile and can cook a wide variety of foods, including vegetables, meats, frozen foods, and even baked goods.
